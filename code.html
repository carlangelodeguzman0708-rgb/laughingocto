Our Story

Small Business,
Big Heart

Hi! I'm a student from Morong, Rizal, and I started this little banana bread hustle to save up for a laptop this summer. Every loaf is baked from scratch in our home kitchen using ripe bananas, real butter, and a whole lot of care.

No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just honest, delicious banana bread made the way it should be — warm from the oven and delivered to you with a smile. 🌻
